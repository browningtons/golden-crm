# Budget and ROI Framework

## Goal
Allocate CRM investments to maximize incremental revenue while protecting channel health and customer trust.

## Budget Buckets

- Always-on lifecycle programs
- Promotional or seasonal pushes
- Experimentation reserve
- Tooling and data quality improvements

## Monthly Allocation Workflow

1. Set monthly budget ceiling and mandatory baseline spend.
2. Allocate 60-70% to proven, scalable programs.
3. Allocate 20-30% to growth opportunities with moderate certainty.
4. Allocate 10% to controlled experiments.
5. Rebalance mid-month based on pacing and observed ROI.

## ROI Model Inputs

- Audience size by segment
- Expected send volume by channel
- Conversion rate assumptions
- Average revenue per conversion
- Channel and creative costs
- Incrementality factor (from holdout tests)

## Decision Rules

- Scale when: incremental ROI exceeds target and guardrails are healthy.
- Hold when: ROI uncertain or confidence interval overlaps neutral.
- Cut when: ROI below threshold for 2 cycles or negative customer signals.

## Risk Controls

- Track opt-outs, complaints, and push disables weekly.
- Enforce frequency caps and suppression rules.
- Reserve budget to recover from deliverability or platform incidents.
