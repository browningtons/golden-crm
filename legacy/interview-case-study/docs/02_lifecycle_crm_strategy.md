# Lifecycle CRM Strategy

## North Star
Build an orchestrated CRM program that increases funded customer conversion while maintaining healthy engagement and brand trust.

## Lifecycle Framework

| Stage | Customer State | Primary Goal | Core Channels | Typical Trigger |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Prospect | New lead, low intent | Qualification and first conversion | Email, SMS | Signup, prequalification started |
| Consideration | Medium intent | Remove friction and strengthen intent | SMS, Email, Push | Application started, no completion |
| Conversion | High intent | Complete approval/funding action | SMS, Push, Email | Decision event, offer event |
| Early Active | New customer | Ensure first successful repayment behavior | Push, Email | First payment due |
| Mature Active | Repeat eligible | Increase repeat utilization and loyalty | Email, Push, SMS | Eligibility refresh, seasonal moments |
| At-Risk | Declining engagement | Win-back with controlled incentives | Email, SMS | Inactivity threshold, missed behavior |

## Channel Roles

- Email: rich education, offer context, compliance-heavy communications, lifecycle nurturing.
- SMS/MMS: high-urgency nudges, reminders, concise conversion prompts.
- Push: behavior-triggered utility and engagement reinforcement in-app.

## Segmentation Model

- Behavioral: recency, funnel step, engagement frequency, inactivity windows.
- Value-based: predicted lifetime value, repayment behavior tier, repeat propensity.
- Eligibility-based: approval status, product eligibility windows.
- Preference-based: channel opt-in status, contact frequency tolerance.

## Cadence and Frequency Guardrails

- Global contact cap per customer per week across channels.
- Suppression logic for recent conversions and recent declines.
- Priority matrix to resolve conflicts between simultaneous campaigns.
- Quiet hours and compliance-safe windows for SMS/push.

## Personalization Priorities

1. Funnel-step-specific copy and CTA.
2. Offer and term relevance by segment.
3. Behavior-aware timing (local time, activity windows).
4. Dynamic content blocks by product relevance.

## KPI Tree

- Business KPI: funded conversion rate, incremental revenue, repeat rate.
- Channel KPI: deliverability, open rate, CTR, CVR, opt-out/uninstall.
- Health KPI: complaint rate, frequency cap breaches, QA defects.
