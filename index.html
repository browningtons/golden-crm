Business Model

What I sell and who I sell it to

Primary Product

A mobile-first co-parent assistant for expense splitting, custody time visibility, payment logging, and shared monthly closeout.

Pricing Strategy

Freemium entry tier for tracking basics, then paid plans for advanced exports, auto-reminders, dispute-ready reports, and attorney/mediator collaboration tools.

Ideal Customer

Divorced or separated parents sharing recurring child-related costs who need a neutral, structured system to reduce conflict.

Revenue Mix

Self-serve subscriptions plus partnership referrals from family-law professionals, therapists, and parenting support communities.

Conversion System

How customers move from lead to buyer

Step 1

Acquire High-Intent Parents

Publish calculators and practical guides like \"How to split shared child expenses fairly,\" then capture leads via web and app stores.

Step 2

Activate in the First Session

Guided onboarding gets both parents to add custody schedule, recurring expenses, and first payment records in under 10 minutes.

Step 3

Convert at First Monthly Close

Free users see their first monthly summary, then unlock premium reporting, automated reminders, and dispute documentation exports.

Retention + Repeat Sales

What I would do after first purchase

Onboarding Flow (Days 0-14)

Triggered email/SMS/push nudges ensure both parents complete profile setup, custody calendar, and first shared expense entry quickly.

Monthly Closeout Loop

Monthly statements summarize who paid what, net balance by parent, and unresolved items, creating a recurring reason to stay.

Reactivation Triggers

If one parent becomes inactive, trigger reminders, simplified re-entry tasks, and guided prompts to restore shared participation.

Expansion Moments

Upsell annual plans, add-on report packs, and professional account access after consistent monthly usage and trust signals.

Weekly Execution

Monday

Tuesday

Campaign Build

Launch + QA

Test Readout

Ship useful educational content, gather customer proof points, and coordinate referral relationships with legal and mediation partners.
